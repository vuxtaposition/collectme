About Me

After working for an Irish shipping company for over 16 years I decided to totally change my career direction. I have always had a love for computers and photography. I returned to college and studied in Dublin City University from 2010 to 2013 and obtained an honours degree in Multimedia. While there I gained experience in programmes like Adobe Photoshop, Dreamweaver, InDesign, Premier Pro, Fireworks, Edge/Animate and Adobe's Soundbooth. I have also used various softwares like Notepad++, Komodo Edit, Cinema 4d, and many other desktop softwares that can manipulate digital media.


This website demonstrates the use of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP and MySQL, it also includes video iframes and audio links embedded with the pages. There is a link to an external mock-up shopping site that uses PHP and MySQL (login is provided for you to take a look). On other sample sites I have used responsive styling in CSS3 that resize the webpage to whatever size your browser is, this works best on iPads, Kindles and other hand-held devices. I also included a photography section that uses a lightbox plugin to allow for a more professional viewing experience.


The video section contains videos I have filmed and edited for a final year college project in DCU. The project was to construct a digital magazine full of rich content specifically for use on the iPad. The target audience for the magazine were up-and-coming Irish Photographers who were interested in photography. Professional photographers like Paul Walsh and Dermot Byrne gave us some tips and advice on how to succeed as a photographer in Ireland.
