Keep safe


Collect me app is an app designed to help parents and guardians keep track of their young children when out and about. The app can track the exact location of the youngster when they are out with friends, at school or in busy public areas like theme parks, carnivals or parades.
Just knowing where you child is at any given time can allow you to have piece of mind and enjoy your time while out and about. The features of collect me app are - Geo location, collect me buttons, pick me up, chat and a Directions and places API provided from Google.
