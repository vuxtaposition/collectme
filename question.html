Career Awareness

Few of us think very far ahead about our lives. Nonetheless, it's worth noting that you are likely to be working for at least 40 to 50 years. That means you will probably be retiring from paid work around 2060! What will the world be like then? How will your working career influence other aspects of your life? What preparations can you make to help you along your journey? Given that we rarely consider these questions, it's worthwhile giving the exercise that follows your full attention.
